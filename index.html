Description

THIS BOOK DOESN’T CONTAIN ANY ARABIC LETTERS! ALL ARABIC WORDS IN THIS BOOK WERE WRITTEN IN ENGLISH-TRANSLITERATION!

 

Would you really want to know how to speak the Arabic Dialect of Qatari but simply didn’t have the time? And if so, then don’t look any further. You can hold one of the most sophisticated and innovative methods in your hands ever developed to quickly become conversational in a language. Master linguist Yatir Nitzany spent years in the development of this time-saving software studying the twenty-seven most popular languages in the world and distilling from them the three hundred and fifty words most likely to be used in real conversations.

 

Those three hundred and fifty words have been chosen in such a way that they are structurally interrelated and form sentences when combined. Via numerous other discoveries about how real conversations work — discoveries that are further outlined in this book — Nitzany developed the required tools to connect these terms together in a specific way so that you can easily and almost instantly become acquainted with them — now. If you want to learn complex grammatical rules or speak Arabic correctly properly and accurately, this book isn’t for you. However, if you just need to have a chat when you are on a trip to Qatar, to please the person, or to be able to speak to your grandfather or grandmother as soon as possible, then the Nitzany Approach is what you were looking for.

 

This book is recommended for those who have any advanced knowledge of Arabic accent pronunciation (such as the Arabic accents: ayin, ghayn, ha, and khaf). This book also offers some fantastic, in-depth strategies on pronunciation and understanding of these accents for those of you who do not, that you can find in the series. Such methods proved to be particularly useful for inexperienced students who had previously been unfamiliar with these accent pronunciations. But note, this is not a book of pronunciations! This approach is conceived for fluency in a foreign language when talking in the present tense first person. Nitzany claims that the most important thing is that another human being will understand and be understood right away. Thus, unlike other classes, all words in this curriculum are taught in English transliteration without the complex alphabet needing to be learned. More formalized instruction will come later in the grammar rules, etc.

 

This is one of many, is a collection of instructional language guides, the innovative approach of the Nitzany Method is the only one in the world that uses its specific language technology to allow you to talk and understand native speakers in as short a time as possible. No more relying on volumes of simple, beginner, intermediate, and advanced books, all with hundreds of pages to learn a language. All you need is forty-six pages, with Conversational Arabic Fast and Simple. Study Arabic today, kick-off now!
